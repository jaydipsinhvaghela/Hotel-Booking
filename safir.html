Safir Camp, Dubaiyah

Home is where you pitch your tent!

Safir Camp, beyond a mere camping ground, it's a space for community bonding and family enjoyment. Our thoughtfully designed desert retreat offers a harmonious blend of comfort, grace, and the enduring beauty of the Dubaiyah desert.

Limited Availability: Book Today.

PYRAMID TENTS

Experience the coziness of our Pyramid tents (4x4 sqm, Regular, and Semi-Private Tents accommodating up to 6 guests) with modern design and comfortable interiors. Sit back on plush cushions and reconnect with nature and each other in a casual-chic setting lit by lanterns within the privacy of your tent.

Our complimentary services include:

> Valet Parking

> Welcome Drink

> Express check-in at the hospitality desk

> Drop-off to the tents

> Personalized assistance is available upon request for a comfortable experience.

> Onsite medical assistance is available in emergencies and is provided by male and female nurses.

Explore our range of Exclusive Add-Ons:

> BBQ Meat for Grilling Box

> Games Box

> Hot Beverages (Tea & Arabic Coffee Package)

> Special Occasion (Birthdays, Girls Night Out, Anniversaries, etc.)

Time & Pricing:

> Sunday to Wednesday: Afternoon 02:00 PM to 02:00 AM (Price KWD 60)

> Thursday to Saturday: Morning 10:00 AM to 02:00 AM (Price KWD 85)
