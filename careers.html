Working with Safir

We are proud of our reputation as Employer of Choice for more than 1,100 Colleagues from around the globe. As an equal opportunity employer committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive workforce, we employ over 45 different nationalities in our hotels & serviced apartments across the MENA region.

When we hire, we look for people who share the same values as us. These values are:

BE PASSIONATE

We are always on the look-out for people who are passionate about providing genuine and intuitive service to our Guests. Professional hoteliers who go the extra mile to create sensational experiences.

HAVE PRIDE

Our team is proud to work in the hospitality industry. We are proud and ambitious. An excellent Guest experience is our ultimate goal.

HAVE INTEGRITY

We say what we do and do as we say, always. We always aim to deliver on our promises to one another and to our Guests. We empower our Colleagues to make their own decisions and deliver excellence as they see fit.

BE CURIOUS

We are always looking for opportunities to improve ourselves, our products and our services. We provide our Colleagues with opportunities to learn and develop their respective careers.
