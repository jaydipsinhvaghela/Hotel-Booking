ABOUT US

Established in Kuwait in 1993 as the first Arabian hospitality management company in the region, Safir Hotels & Resorts manages Upscale and Midscale hotels & serviced apartments in the Middle East and North Africa. We are commitment to excellence, and dutifully adhere to global best practices, high-quality hotel and hospitality management standards, and to our purpose of "Sharing True Arabian Hospitality with the World."
