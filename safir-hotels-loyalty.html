Safir Hotels & Resorts Unveils "Durrah Rewards" – A New Era in Guest Loyalty and Hospitality Excellence

Feb 15. 2025

Safir Hotels & Resorts proudly announces the launch of 'Durrah Rewards,' an innovative loyalty program crafted to elevate guest experiences across the company's distinguished portfolio of properties. This program underscores Safir's commitment to providing unparalleled value, flexibility, and personalized service to its esteemed guests.

The launch of Durrah Rewards marks a significant milestone in Safir Hotels & Resorts' ongoing pursuit of excellence and dedication to enhancing guest satisfaction. The program offers an array of exclusive benefits, recognizing and rewarding loyal guests at every interaction. With a comprehensive range of perks and privileges, members can expect to enjoy a more enriched and seamless stay at any Safir Hotels & Resorts location.

In a statement reflecting on this exciting development, Abdulaziz Al Failakawi, Vice Chairman and CEO of Safir Hotels & Resorts, remarked, "Durrah Rewards represents our unwavering commitment to redefining hospitality standards and fostering deeper connections with our guests. This strategic initiative not only enhances guest satisfaction but also reaffirms our position as a leader in the region's hospitality sector. Our guests are at the heart of everything we do, and Durrah Rewards is our way of expressing gratitude and appreciation for their continued loyalty."

Durrah Rewards introduces a host of exclusive discounts and services tailored to provide exceptional value to its members. From Member-only Rates that offer special discounts when booking directly through the Safir Hotels & Resorts website or direct reservation channels to reduced rates on in-room dining, the program ensures unparalleled savings and convenience. Members can also enjoy discounts on food and beverages across all outlets within the participating Safir properties, from fine dining restaurants to casual cafes, as well as discounted airport transfers for seamless and stress-free transportation. With a focus on flexibility and personalization, Durrah Rewards offers four tiered membership levels—Base, Silver, Gold, and Diamond—each unlocking greater privileges and rewarding guests for their loyalty and engagement with Safir Hotels & Resorts.

A Unique Point Sharing Experience:

A standout feature of Durrah Rewards is the ability for members to share their points with friends and family. This unique aspect of the program allows guests to gift points, enabling their loved ones to enjoy the benefits of the program as well. By creating this sense of community and shared experiences, Safir Hotels & Resorts further strengthens the bonds between the brand and its loyal clientele.

Driving Innovation and Guest-Centric Initiatives:

As part of its forward-thinking approach, Safir Hotels & Resorts has consistently invested in creating personalized and meaningful experiences for guests. Durrah Rewards is a testament to this spirit, reflecting the company's broader strategy to enhance operational efficiency, performance management, and quality assurance. By placing guests at the center of its operations, Safir Hotels & Resorts continues to set new benchmarks in the industry.

Join Durrah Rewards Today:

Enrollment in Durrah Rewards is now open to all guests, providing immediate access to exclusive benefits and privileges. Those interested in becoming members can easily sign up through the Safir Hotels & Resorts website or by visiting any Safir property.

For further details about Durrah Rewards and to explore the full list of benefits, please visit safirhotels.com/durrah-rewards
